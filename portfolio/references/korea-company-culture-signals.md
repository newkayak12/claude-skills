# Korea Company Culture Signals — Resume Writing Reference

Shared reference for portfolio/ skills (resume-tailorer, portfolio-rewrite, etc.).
Use this to adjust the tone, vocabulary, and emphasis of resume content based on the target company type.

---

## Chaebol / Large Korean Conglomerate (삼성, LG, SK, 현대 계열사)

**Emphasize:**
- Cross-functional coordination and stakeholder management
- Process adherence and documentation rigor
- Long-term project ownership and sustained contribution
- Team harmony and collaborative decision-making
- Formal communication and escalation patterns

**Deemphasize:**
- "Fast and break things" language
- Individual heroics or going around process
- Frequent job switching framed as agility

**Language to use:** "협업", "체계적", "안정적 운영", "팀 기여", "프로세스 개선", "조직 내 조율"

---

## Korean Tech Unicorn / Scale-up (카카오, 네이버, 쿠팡, 토스, 당근마켓 등)

**Emphasize:**
- Scale of systems operated (DAU, TPS, data volume — use real numbers)
- Technical depth and ownership of design decisions
- Measurable, data-driven impact
- Speed of execution alongside quality

**Deemphasize:**
- Process-heavy framing without results
- Collective ownership language that obscures individual contribution

**Language to use:** "오너십", "직접 설계", "데이터 기반", "병목 개선", specific scale numbers (e.g., "일 활성 사용자 150만")

---

## Global Tech in Korea (Google Korea, Meta, Amazon, Microsoft Korea, LINE Corp 글로벌 팀)

**Emphasize:**
- Individual contribution made explicit — distinguish "I" from "we"
- Scope and complexity of technical decisions
- Cross-timezone or cross-geo collaboration
- Impact framed in global or organizational terms, not just team terms

**Deemphasize:**
- Collective language that obscures personal impact
- Purely local Korean-market framing if the role is global

**Language:** English technical terms are acceptable and often expected; impact framed globally where possible.
Mixed Korean/English is standard for these roles.

---

## Early-Stage Startup (시리즈 A 이하, 초기 스타트업)

**Emphasize:**
- Breadth across functions — full-stack, infra, product thinking
- "Zero to one" stories: things that did not exist before you built them
- Self-direction and initiative without management overhead
- Speed: how quickly did you ship, iterate, learn?

**Deemphasize:**
- Team size and elaborate org structure (signals over-engineering)
- Process compliance as an achievement

**Language to use:** "초기 설계", "직접 구축", "빠른 반복", "혼자서", "0에서 1로"

---

## Usage Notes

- These signals apply to resume vocabulary, summary framing, and achievement reframing.
- When a JD contains mixed signals (e.g., a chaebol subsidiary running like a startup), use the
  JD's own language to arbitrate — JD wording always overrides the category default.
- For ATS keyword matching rules, see `ats-rules-korea.md` in this same directory.
